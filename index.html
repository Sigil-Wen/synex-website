About Synex

Synex is developing the next generation of biosensor technology. We’re building a highly miniaturized system that can non-invasively measure critical metabolites in your body in real-time. These include things like glucose, cholesterol, ketones, and more.
Our vision is to provide people with constant and non-invasive data about how their bodies are functioning to help make healthcare more predictive and allow people to be proactive about their health.
Our technology is based on magnetic resonance, the same underlying principle as in MRI. We’ve developed novel techniques that allow us to measure highly accurate magnetic resonance signals in an ultra-compact form-factor.
